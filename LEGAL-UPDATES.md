## 1. Privacy Policy — Yeni Bölüm: In-App Purchases / Premium

### English (EN)

**10. In-App Purchases (KlickZeit Pro)**

KlickZeit offers an optional premium upgrade called **KlickZeit Pro**:

- **Product type:** One-time lifetime purchase (non-subscription)
- **What it includes:** Removes banner advertisements from the app
- **Payment:** Processed by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android); we do not collect or store your payment card details
- **Price:** Displayed on the in-app paywall before you confirm purchase
- **Purchase management:** Use **Settings → Manage Purchases** in the app to restore purchases on a new device

We use **RevenueCat** (RevenueCat, Inc.) to manage purchase validation and entitlements. RevenueCat may process transaction identifiers and entitlement status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Your work time records and account data are **not sold** and are not used to build advertising profiles. Purchase data is limited to what is required to verify your Pro status.
